About Infinite Shelf

Infinite Shelf is a student-focused startup created to make engineering study materials more affordable and accessible. Many students struggle to buy expensive textbooks and lab equipment, so our platform provides second-hand engineering books and essential academic tools at lower prices.

Our Mission

  • Make engineering study materials affordable for students
  • Encourage reuse of second-hand books
  • Provide essential lab and academic equipment in one place
  • Support engineering students throughout their academic journey

What We Offer

  • Second-hand engineering textbooks
  • Scientific calculators
  • Lab coats for practical sessions
  • EGD (Engineering Graphics) kits

Our goal is to help engineering students save money while still getting the resources they need to succeed in their studies.
